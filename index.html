How Order Matching Works 订单匹配机制

Orders are matched by timestamp (second precision) and amount. Same-second orders take priority, with exact amount matching first. One order can match multiple counterparties. 5% entry fee is deducted immediately upon successful matching, and 5% exit fee upon contract expiration. All profits and losses are calculated after fee deductions. 订单按时间戳（精确到秒）和金额匹配。同一秒的订单优先，优先匹配金额完全对等的订单。一个订单可以与多个对手方匹配。匹配成功后立即扣除5%入场手续费，合约到期时扣除5%出场手续费。所有盈亏均在扣除手续费后计算。
